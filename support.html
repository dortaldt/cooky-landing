‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a recipe?

You can add recipes in three ways:

  • Import from URL: Paste a recipe link and let AI extract the details
  • Take a Photo: Snap a picture of a recipe card or cookbook page
  • Manual Entry: Type in your recipe manually

How accurate is the nutritional information?

Nutritional information is calculated using AI and industry-standard databases. While we strive for accuracy, values are estimates and should not be used for medical purposes. Always consult with a healthcare professional for dietary advice.

Can I adjust serving sizes?

Yes! Tap on any recipe and use the serving size adjuster. All ingredients and nutritional information will automatically scale.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Email dortaldt@gmail.com
  2. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your account email address
  4. Your account will be deleted within 30 days

Is my data safe?

Yes! Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider with enterprise-grade security. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Does Cooky work offline?

Currently, Cooky requires an internet connection to sync your recipes and use AI features. Offline support may be added in future updates.

Can I export my recipes?

Yes! Contact us at dortaldt@gmail.com to request a data export. We'll send you all your recipes in a machine-readable format.

Is Cooky free?

Yes! Cooky is currently free to use with all features included. We may introduce premium features in the future, but core functionality will always remain free.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • App won't load: Check your internet connection and try restarting the app
  • Login issues: Verify your email and password, or use "Forgot Password"
  • Photos won't upload: Check camera permissions in your device settings
  • Slow performance: Try closing other apps and restarting your device
  • Sync issues: Make sure you have a stable internet connection

System Requirements

  • Android: Android 6.0 or later
  • Web: Modern browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
